A Tribute Page

Manga Artist Fujiko Fujio

A man who created Doraemon

Here is a timeline of Fujiko's life:

  • 1933 - Born in Toyama Prefecture
  • 1951 - Made publishing debut with Motoo Abiko
  • 1954 - Moved to Tokyo as professional manga artist
  • 1956 - Formed Shin Manga-To with Fujio Akatsuka and Ishinomori Shotaro
  • 1962 - Got married at age of 28 in Kawasaki
  • 1963 - Established Studio Zero with Abiko and other artists
  • 1969 - Created Doraemon which is popular all over the world nowadays
  • 1982 - Received Shogakukan Manga Award for children's manga
  • 1987 - Ended long partnership with Abiko to concentrate on solo projects
  • 1989 - Received Golden Gloss prize
  • 1996 - Passed away due to cancer

You can find more about Fujiko Juio on his Wikipedia
